How our FOCO Model Guarantees 20%+ Yield

The FOCO model (Franchise Owned, Company Operated) is the backbone of Studios By Ritumbhara's success. But what does it actually mean for you as a property owner? Let's dive into why this model is a game-changer for rental yields.

Operational Excellence

We take over 100% of the daily operations. This isn't just about cleaning and key handovers; it's about revenue management, guest screening, and maintenance scheduling. By operating at scale, we bring efficiencies that individual owners simply can't match.

Dynamic Pricing Algorithms

Static pricing is a relic of the past. Our team uses advanced data tools to adjust your property's daily rates based on local demand, events, and seasonality. This ensures you're never leaving money on the table.

When you list with Studios By Ritumbhara, you're not just another Airbnb listing. You're part of a curated collection known for quality. This brand trust translates directly into higher click-through rates and more bookings.
