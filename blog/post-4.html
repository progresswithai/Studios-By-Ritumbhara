Interior Design Tips for 5-Star Reviews

In the world of short-term rentals, first impressions aren't just important—they're everything. A well-designed interior can be the difference between a one-star rating and a glowing five-star review.

Lighting is Key

Avoid harsh overhead lights. Use warm, layered lighting with lamps and sconces to create a cozy, premium feel. Guests love an atmosphere that feels intentional.

Texture and Comfort

Invest in high-quality linens and throw pillows. Textures add depth to a space and make it feel more luxurious in photos—which helps increase your booking rates.

Functional Minimalism

Don't clutter the space. In a studio, every piece of furniture must have a purpose. Maintain a clean, open layout that allows guests to move freely.
